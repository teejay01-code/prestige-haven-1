About Us


Welcome to Prestige Havens – Your Home Away from Home

Nestled in the heart of Alagbaka, Akure, Ondo State, a minute drive from from the popular dome event centre and two minutes drive to Akure's Shoprite and the Governor's Office, Prestige Havens is a premium apartment-style hotel designed for comfort, luxury, and convenience. Whether you are visiting for business, leisure, or a long-term stay, we offer a serene retreat that combines world-class hospitality with the warmth of home.

Our hotel is strategically located, providing easy access to major attractions, business districts, and local culture. With a commitment to excellence, we ensure that every guest enjoys a unique experience tailored to their needs.

At Prestige Havens, we believe that where you stay should be more than just a place to rest, — it should be an experience. Our elegantly furnished apartments provide a perfect blend of sophistication and relaxation, ensuring you feel right at home from the moment you step in. From spacious living areas to modern amenities, every detail is curated to enhance your stay.


Why Choose Prestige Havens?

Prime Location – Situated in the upscale Alagbaka district of Akure, our hotel provides easy access to key attractions, business hubs, government offices, and entertainment centers.

Luxurious Accommodations – Our stylishly designed apartments offer a choice of standard rooms, deluxe suites, and executive apartments, all equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi, plush bedding, smart TVs, and fully fitted kitchenettes.

Exceptional Hospitality – Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to delivering personalized service, ensuring every guest enjoys a seamless and memorable experience.

Modern Facilities – Enjoy top-tier amenities, including a 24-hour front desk, housekeeping services, on-site dining, conference rooms, fitness center, and secure parking.

Safety & Security – Your safety is our priority. Prestige Havens is equipped with round-the-clock security, CCTV surveillance, and key card access for peace of mind.

frequently asked questions

Where is Prestige Haven located?

Prestige Haven is situated at House 7, Road 2, Court of Appeal Estate, in the heart of Akure, Ondo State. Our central location offers guests convenient access to local attractions, business centers, and cultural sites.

Does the hotel provide free Wi-Fi?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, including all guest rooms and public areas, ensuring you stay connected during your visit.

How can I make a reservation at Prestige Haven?

Reservations can be made through our official website, by contacting our reservations team via phone at +2348062451893, or by emailing us at prestigeapartment9@gmail.com. We recommend booking directly to enjoy exclusive offers and the best rates.

Is Prestige Haven suitable for families?

Yes, Prestige Haven is a family-friendly hotel. We provide amenities such as family suites, baby cots, and babysitting services upon request. Our staff is dedicated to ensuring a comfortable stay for guests of all ages.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including cash, debit/credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), and bank transfers. For corporate bookings, we also accommodate invoicing arrangements. Please contact our accounts department for more details.

What room types are available at Prestige Haven?

We offer a variety of room categories to suit different preferences and budgets, including:
• Standard Rooms: Cozy and well-appointed for a comfortable stay.
• Deluxe Rooms: Spacious rooms with enhanced amenities. • Executive Suites: Luxurious suites featuring separate living areas and premium services.
• Family Suites: Designed for families, offering ample space and child-friendly amenities.

Is room service available?

Yes, we provide 24-hour room service, allowing you to enjoy meals and refreshments in the comfort of your room at any time.

Is on-site parking available?

Yes, we provide complimentary secure parking for all our guests. Our parking area is monitored 24/7 to ensure the safety of your vehicle.
